About Us

Reco Automotive Group, a leading multi-location automobile dealership group, currently operates 18 franchised dealerships, comprised of 16 brands, in Illinois and employs over 600 employees.


Reco Automotive Group consists of eight metro dealerships, selling and servicing nine different brands: Chevrolet, Chrysler, Dodge, Honda, Jeep, Hyundai, KIA, Toyota & RAM. It also includes seven luxury and premium brands in two auto malls in Bloomington/Normal, Illinois, and Peoria, Illinois, including Audi, Lincoln, Mercedes, Porsche, Subaru, Volkswagen, and Volvo. The multi-location dealership model enables us to serve our client base, providing a range of services to customers, including new and used vehicle sales, financing, leasing, automotive service and repair, collision repair, and parts sales.


RECO – Reco Rewards – is the name of Reco Automotive Group’s exclusive member rewards program. It is simple to use and provides rewards, exclusive offers, seasonal specials and unique experiences for members.
